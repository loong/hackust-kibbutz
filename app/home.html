Welcome to Kibbutz!

We plan to revolutionize the way charity functions. Our technology allows to improve the functioning of charities in 3 major ways:

  • Donors have a very transparent view of what happens with the money they have donated
  • They have a direct impact on what happens with their money thanks to voting power that they receive depending on the size of the donation
  • You can respond to catastrophes much faster as projects can be created and financed in a matter of seconds
